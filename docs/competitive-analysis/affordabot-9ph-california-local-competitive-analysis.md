## Platform Description
California Local is developing AI and big-data tools to help citizens participate in local governance by managing the enormous amount of data produced by local governments. The goal is to leverage AI to sift and summarize information, making knowledge citizens need easier and faster to find.

## Core Features

### Data Processing
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Uses AI to process large volumes of local government data
- **Big Data Tools**: Manages data from city councils and county boards
- **Information Summarization**: Extracts key information for citizen consumption
- **Accessibility Focus**: Makes civic knowledge more accessible

### Coverage Area
- **10 California Counties**: Experience building database for multiple counties
- **Local Governments**: City councils, county boards of supervisors
- **Data Sources**: Comprehensive local government data collection

### Research Foundation
- **Stanford Connection**: Tools developed at Stanford University's Big Local News
- **Academic Rigor**: Research-based approach to civic technology
- **Journalism Focus**: Supporting resource-strapped local newsrooms

## Target Markets
- **Citizens**: General public wanting to understand local government
- **Local Newsrooms**: Journalists covering local government
- **Civic Organizations**: Groups needing government information
- **Researchers**: Academic and policy researchers

## Business Model
- **Public Service**: Likely grant or foundation funded
- **Non-profit Mission**: Focus on civic good over profit
- **Open Source**: May share tools and research publicly
