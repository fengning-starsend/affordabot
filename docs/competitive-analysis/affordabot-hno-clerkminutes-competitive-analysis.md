## Platform Description
ClerkMinutes is an AI-powered meeting minutes software specifically designed for municipal clerks to automate the process of creating meeting minutes. The platform eliminates the long hours clerks spend transcribing and formatting minutes, allowing them to leave work on time.

## Core Features

### AI Transcription
- **98% Speaker Accuracy**: High-accuracy transcription with complex motion detection
- **Multiple Format Support**: MP3, WAV, MOV, AVI, Zoom, Vimeo, YouTube imports
- **20-Minute Processing**: From upload to review in about 20 minutes
- **Speaker Identification**: Automatic detection and assignment of speakers

### Minutes Generation
- **Template Matching**: Formats to match municipality's specific style
- **Motion Detection**: Captures complex motions and amendments
- **Overlap Handling**: Manages multiple speakers talking simultaneously
- **Custom Formatting**: Adapts to each municipality's template requirements

### Platform Capabilities
- **All-in-One Workflow**: Agenda, audio, and minutes in one system
- **No Training Required**: Drag-and-drop interface
- **Editing Tools**: Polish transcripts and fix names/context
- **Export Options**: Multiple format downloads for sharing

## Target Markets
- **Municipal Clerks**: Primary users responsible for meeting documentation
- **Local Governments**: Cities, towns, villages needing minutes automation
- **Meeting Administrators**: Anyone managing official meeting records
- **Government Staff**: City managers, administrators

## Pricing Structure
- **SaaS Model**: Likely subscription-based per municipality
- **Time Savings Value**: Based on 5+ hours saved per meeting
- **No Public Pricing**: Custom quotes for municipalities

## Customer Base
- **637 Municipalities Served**: Established customer base
- **City of Providence, UT**: Public testimonial of time reduction from 7-8 hours to 2-3 hours
