## Platform Description
HeyGov is a digital government toolkit designed to help municipalities work better and faster. The platform offers four integrated tools to help local government employees and constituents manage everyday tasks digitally, aiming to save time, generate revenue, and enable compliance.

## Core Features

### HeyLicense (Primary Product)
- **Digital Applications**: Online submission of forms and permits
- **License Management**: Digital licensing for various municipal services
- **Workflow Customization**: Tailored workflows for each municipality
- **Integration Capabilities**: Works with existing municipal systems

### Additional Modules
- **HeyPay**: Accept card payments online and in person
- **Hey311**: Report and track community issues
- **HeyReserve**: Manage and book municipal facilities

### Platform Capabilities
- **All-in-One Solution**: Comprehensive municipal digital toolkit
- **Mobile App**: Available on Google Play for citizens
- **Customizable**: Adapts to each municipality's specific needs
- **Paperless**: Helps municipalities go digital

## Target Markets
- **Small to Medium Municipalities**: Cities, towns, villages
- **Municipal Employees**: Clerks, administrators, department heads
- **Citizens**: Residents needing municipal services
- **Local Government**: Various municipal departments

## Pricing Structure
- **SaaS Model**: Subscription-based pricing per municipality
- **Affordable Focus**: Designed to be accessible for small municipalities
- **No Public Pricing**: Custom quotes based on municipality size

## Known Achievements
- **Wisconsin Governor's Business Plan**: First place in Information Technology category
- **Multiple Module Strategy**: Four integrated tools for comprehensive coverage

## Competitive Analysis vs. AffordaBot

### Overlap Areas
- Both serve local government entities
- Both aim to improve civic processes
- Both use technology to make government more accessible
- Both focus on digital transformation

### Key Differences
| Dimension | HeyGov | AffordaBot |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Primary Focus** | Municipal operations digitization | Cost-of-living impact |
| **Target User** | Municipal employees | Families, voters |
| **Output Type** | Digital workflows/tools | Dollar impact analysis |
| **Business Model** | B2B SaaS for municipalities | B2C insights platform |
| **Technical Focus** | Workflow automation | Economic modeling |
| **Value Proposition** | Government efficiency vs family financial impact |

## Competitive Threat Level: VERY LOW

### Reasons for Very Low Threat
1. **Different Target Market**: Municipal employees vs families
2. **Different Value Proposition**: Operational efficiency vs personal impact
3. **B2B vs B2C**: Government contracts vs consumer platform
4. **Technical Focus**: Workflow automation vs economic analysis
5. **Integration vs Competition**: Natural integration opportunity
