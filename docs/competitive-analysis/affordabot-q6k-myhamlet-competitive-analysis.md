## Platform Description
MyHamlet provides local government intelligence by monitoring public meetings across hundreds of US jurisdictions. The platform extracts actionable insights from city councils, counties, commissions, and other local government bodies for professionals in real estate, public affairs, and government administration.

## Core Features

### Meeting Intelligence
- **Agenda Monitoring**: Track meeting agendas and topics
- **Project Data Feeds**: Follow specific projects and developments
- **Quote Search**: Full-text search across meeting recordings
- **Jurisdiction Coverage**: Hundreds of US jurisdictions monitored

### Platform Capabilities
- **Real-time Updates**: Live monitoring of government meetings
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Extract key insights and quotes
- **Professional Focus**: Designed for professional users
- **Custom Alerts**: Notifications on specific topics of interest

## Target Markets
- **Real Estate Professionals**: Development opportunities, zoning changes
- **Public Affairs Strategists**: Policy monitoring and analysis
- **Government Administrators**: Cross-jurisdictional intelligence
- **Legal Professionals**: Regulatory compliance and tracking

## Pricing Structure
- **Enterprise Model**: Custom pricing for organizations
- **Professional Focus**: B2B subscription model
- **No Public Pricing**: Enterprise sales only

## Known Deployments
- **Saratoga, CA**: Active deployment with AI-powered City Council summaries
- **GovCenter Portals**: Assisting cities with dedicated civic information hubs
